// About

Fractional Leadership.
AI-Powered Execution.

ITBG Labs is a growth strategy and consulting practice led by Blake Hall, built on 10+ years of scaling startups across gaming, sweepstakes, fintech, blockchain, and consumer tech.

Through ITBG Labs, I've helped early-stage and scaling companies define growth strategy, build full-stack marketing engines, and prepare for key inflection points including funding rounds and acquisitions. Notable work includes repositioning a dying Daily Fantasy Sports platform (Moneyball) into an Australian licensed sportsbook that was ultimately acquired by Dabble.

I specialise in turning ambiguity into strategy: taking companies from "we know we need growth but don't know where to start" to a clear roadmap their team can execute on. My role is to compress years of learning into weeks. Your team does the building, but they build the right things, in the right order, without the expensive mistakes.

My experience spans regulated markets, high-growth verticals, and industries where compliance, speed, and creative problem-solving all matter. I work with a small number of clients at a time to ensure every engagement gets senior-level attention and real strategic value.

The Approach

Methodical and data-driven. I diagnose growth challenges, identify the real constraints, and map out exactly what to do about them, in what order. No generic playbooks.

The Difference

Most consultants hand you a strategy doc and disappear. I stay close. I guide your team through execution, course-correct in real time, and make sure the strategy actually becomes reality.

The Shortcut

I've already made the expensive mistakes across dozens of startups. Your team gets the benefit of that pattern recognition so they can skip the learning curve and move faster.
