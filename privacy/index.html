Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 February 2026

ITBG Labs Pty Ltd (ACN 662 560 947) ("ITBG Labs", "we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs). This policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you visit our website at itbglabs.com or engage with our services.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

When you submit our contact form, we collect:

  • Your first and last name
  • Your work email address
  • The content of your inquiry message
  • Your consent preferences (data collection consent and optional marketing consent)

Information collected automatically

When you visit our website, certain information is collected automatically through cookies and similar technologies:

  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website, including pages visited, time spent, traffic sources, device type, and approximate geographic location. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect this data. You can opt out via the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
  • Meta Pixel (Facebook): We use Meta Pixel to measure the effectiveness of our advertising and to understand actions visitors take on our website. This may involve sharing data with Meta Platforms, Inc. You can manage your ad preferences at Meta Ad Preferences.
  • TikTok Pixel: We use TikTok Pixel for advertising measurement purposes. Data may be shared with TikTok (ByteDance). You can manage your preferences via TikTok's privacy settings.
  • Microsoft Clarity: We use Microsoft Clarity for session recording and heatmap analysis to understand how visitors use our site. Clarity captures anonymised interaction data including mouse movements, clicks, and scrolling behaviour. Learn more at Clarity's terms.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use personal information to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and communicate about our services
  • Send marketing communications (only if you have opted in)
  • Analyse website traffic and improve our website experience
  • Measure the performance of our advertising
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data with:

  • FormSubmit.co: Our contact form submissions are processed through FormSubmit.co, which handles form delivery via email. Your form data passes through their servers.
  • Analytics and advertising platforms: As described above (Google, Meta, TikTok, Microsoft), aggregated or pseudonymous data is shared for analytics and advertising purposes.
  • Email service providers: If you opt in to marketing communications, your email may be processed by our email delivery provider.
  • Professional advisers: Accountants, lawyers, or auditors where required.
  • Regulatory authorities: Where required by law.

4. International Data Transfers

Some of the third-party services we use (Google, Meta, TikTok, Microsoft, FormSubmit.co) are operated by companies based outside Australia, primarily in the United States. By using our website and submitting your information, you acknowledge that your data may be transferred to and processed in jurisdictions outside Australia. We take reasonable steps to ensure that overseas recipients handle your personal information in compliance with the APPs.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this policy or as required by law. Contact form submissions are retained for the duration of any potential or active business relationship. You may request deletion at any time.

6. Cookies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies. These include:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function (e.g., form submission)
  • Analytics cookies: Google Analytics, Microsoft Clarity
  • Advertising cookies: Meta Pixel, TikTok Pixel

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect your experience on our website.

7. Your Rights

Under the Australian Privacy Act, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Withdraw consent to marketing communications at any time
  • Complain to the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) if you believe your privacy has been breached

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, you may also have rights under the GDPR, including the right to data portability, the right to restrict processing, and the right to object to processing.

8. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information from misuse, interference, loss, unauthorised access, modification, and disclosure. Our website is served over HTTPS and protected by Cloudflare. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.
